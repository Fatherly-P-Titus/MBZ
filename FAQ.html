About TBZ Global

TBZ Global is a leading pure water bottling company dedicated to providing premium-quality drinking water while prioritizing environmental sustainability.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the bottled water industry, we combine state-of-the-art purification technology with eco-conscious packaging solutions.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional hydration while minimizing our environmental footprint through innovative practices and community engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • filter_1What makes TBZ water different?

    TBZ Global water undergoes a rigorous 7-step purification process that includes reverse osmosis, UV treatment, and mineral enhancement. Our water exceeds all industry purity standards while maintaining essential minerals for taste and health benefits.

  • filter_2Are your bottles recyclable?

    Yes, all our bottles are 100% recyclable. We use PET plastic that is widely accepted in recycling programs. Additionally, our bottles contain 30-40% recycled materials, depending on the size, as part of our commitment to circular economy principles.

  • filter_3Where is TBZ water sourced?

    We source our water from protected underground aquifers that are regularly tested for purity. Before bottling, the water undergoes our advanced purification process to ensure consistent quality and taste.

  • filter_4Do you offer bulk ordering?

    Yes, we offer bulk ordering options for businesses, events, and institutions. Please contact our sales team through the Contact Us page for customized bulk order pricing and delivery options.

  • filter_5How can I become a distributor?

    We're always looking to expand our distribution network. Interested parties can submit a distributor application through our contact page. Our team will review your application and contact you within 5 business days.
